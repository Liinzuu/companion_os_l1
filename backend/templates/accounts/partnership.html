If you work with people in difficult situations and think Companion OS belongs in what you offer them. Organizations, rehabilitation services, reentry programs, schools, healthcare providers, funders, researchers. If the fit is right, I want to hear from you.

What Companion OS is: an educational cognitive support tool that teaches practical coping skills (panic, cravings, cognitive distortions, self-criticism, planning, decision-making, boundaries, communication, study and reading support, daily anchoring). It is not therapy and not a medical device. It does not diagnose, treat, or monitor any condition. Privacy and security are first-class concerns throughout the build.

Right now Companion is in a small invite-only individual-user pilot. The next pilot wave is planned for organizations: 30-50 users hosted through a partner organization that already has the trust relationship with the people the tool is built for. If that fits what you do, I would like to hear from you.
